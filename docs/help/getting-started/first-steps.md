# First Steps with GigaChad GRC

Welcome to GigaChad GRC! This guide will help you get productive in your first 30 minutes on the platform.

## Before You Begin

Ensure you have:
- Your login credentials (SSO or direct login)
- Your assigned role and permissions
- Access to the modules relevant to your work

## Step 1: Explore the Dashboard (2 minutes)

After logging in, you'll land on the **Dashboard**. This provides:

- **Compliance Score**: Your overall compliance health
- **Quick Stats**: Controls, policies, evidence items
- **Framework Readiness**: Progress toward compliance frameworks
- **Recent Activity**: Latest updates and changes

### Quick Actions
Use the Quick Actions panel on the right to:
- Create a new control
- Add a risk
- Upload evidence

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile (3 minutes)

Click your name in the bottom-left corner and select **Account Settings**:

1. **Profile Photo**: Upload a photo for easy identification
2. **Notification Preferences**: Configure how you receive alerts
3. **Time Zone**: Ensure dates display correctly

## Step 3: Understand the Navigation (5 minutes)

The left sidebar organizes features into modules:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview and metrics |
| **Compliance** | Controls, frameworks, calendar |
| **Data** | Evidence, policies, assets, integrations |
| **Risk Management** | Risk register, heatmaps, scenarios |
| **Third Party Risk** | Vendors, assessments, contracts |
| **People** | Employee compliance, training |
| **Trust** | Public trust center, knowledge base |
| **Audit** | Internal/external audits |
| **BC/DR** | Business continuity planning |
| **Tools** | Reports, training programs |
| **Settings** | Configuration and administration |

## Step 4: Complete Your First Task (10 minutes)

### Option A: If you're in Compliance
1. Go to **Compliance → Controls**
2. Click **Add Control** to create your first control
3. Fill in the title, description, and assign an owner
4. Set the implementation status

### Option B: If you're in Risk Management
1. Go to **Risk Management → Risk Register**
2. Click **Add Risk** to report a new risk
3. Describe the risk and estimate severity
4. Submit for assessment

### Option C: If you're managing Vendors
1. Go to **Third Party Risk → Vendors**
2. Click **Add Vendor** to register a vendor
3. Enter vendor details and risk tier
4. Initiate a security assessment

## Step 5: Explore the Help Center (5 minutes)

Click **Help Center** in the top navigation bar to access:
- Step-by-step guides for every feature
- Video tutorials (if available)
- FAQ and troubleshooting

## Step 6: Join Your Team (5 minutes)

Check with your administrator to:
- Join the correct **Workspace** (if multi-workspace is enabled)
- Be added to the appropriate **Permission Group**
- Receive any pending task assignments

## Tips for Success

1. **Use the Search**: Press `⌘K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) to search everything
2. **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Press `⌘/` to see available shortcuts
3. **Bulk Upload**: Use CSV import for adding multiple items at once
4. **Stay Notified**: Configure notification preferences for important updates
