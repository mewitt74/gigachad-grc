# Getting Started with GigaChad GRC

Welcome to GigaChad GRC! This guide will help you get up and running quickly with your governance, risk, and compliance platform.

## Quick Start Checklist

Before diving in, here's what you'll accomplish:

- [ ] Complete your organization profile
- [ ] Invite team members
- [ ] Connect your first integration
- [ ] Set up a compliance framework
- [ ] Create your first control
- [ ] Configure your Trust Center

## Step 1: Complete Your Organization Profile

1. Navigate to **Settings** â†’ **Organization**
2. Fill in your organization details:
   - Company name
   - Industry
   - Company size
   - Primary contact information
3. Upload your company logo (used in Trust Center and reports)
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Step 2: Invite Your Team

GigaChad GRC is designed for collaboration. Invite your team members:

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Users**
2. Click **Invite User**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select a role:
   - **Admin**: Full platform access, can manage users and settings
   - **Editor**: Can create and modify content, cannot manage users
   - **Viewer**: Read-only access to all areas
   - **Auditor**: Special read access optimized for audit reviews
5. Click **Send Invitation**

## Step 3: Connect Your First Integration

Integrations automate evidence collection from your existing tools.

### Recommended First Integrations

| Priority | Integration | Why |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| 1 | Cloud Provider (AWS/GCP/Azure) | Provides infrastructure security evidence |
| 2 | Identity Provider (Okta/Azure AD) | Shows user access management |
| 3 | Version Control (GitHub/GitLab) | Demonstrates secure development practices |
| 4 | HR System (BambooHR/Workday) | Powers employee compliance tracking |

### How to Connect

1. Navigate to **Integrations**
2. Find your integration in the list
3. Click **Configure**
4. Enter your API credentials (see integration-specific guides for details)
5. Select which evidence types to collect
6. Set a sync frequency
7. Click **Save & Connect**

## Step 4: Set Up a Compliance Framework

Frameworks define your compliance requirements:

1. Go to **Frameworks**
2. Click **Add Framework**
3. Choose a framework:
   - **SOC 2 Type II** (most common for SaaS)
   - **ISO 27001**
   - **HIPAA** (healthcare)
   - **PCI DSS** (payments)
   - **GDPR** (EU data privacy)
4. Review the imported controls
5. Click **Activate Framework**

## Step 5: Create Your First Control

Controls are the building blocks of compliance:

1. Navigate to **Controls**
2. Click **Create Control**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Control ID**: A unique identifier (e.g., AC-001)
   - **Title**: Brief name (e.g., "Multi-Factor Authentication Required")
   - **Description**: What this control does
   - **Category**: Area of compliance (Access Control, Data Security, etc.)
4. Map to framework requirements
5. Assign an owner
6. Click **Create Control**

## Step 6: Configure Your Trust Center

Your Trust Center is your public-facing security portal:

1. Go to **Trust Center** â†’ **Settings**
2. Customize branding:
   - Upload your logo
   - Set primary colors
   - Add a welcome message
3. Choose what to share:
   - Compliance certifications
   - Security documentation
   - Penetration test summaries
4. Enable your Trust Center
5. Share the link with customers and prospects
