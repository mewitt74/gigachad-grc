# AI Risk Assistant

The AI Risk Assistant helps you analyze and manage risks using artificial intelligence.

## Overview

The AI Risk Assistant provides:
- Automated risk categorization
- Risk score recommendations
- Treatment suggestions
- Similar risk identification
- Natural language queries

## Accessing AI Features

Navigate to **Risk Management → AI Risk Assistant**

Or access contextually:
- From risk detail pages
- During risk creation
- In risk queue

## AI Capabilities

### Risk Categorization
AI analyzes risk descriptions to suggest:
- Risk category
- Threat type
- Impact areas
- Related frameworks

### Risk Scoring
AI recommends likelihood and impact:
- Based on risk description
- Industry benchmarks
- Historical data
- Similar risk analysis

### Treatment Recommendations
AI suggests treatment options:
- Mitigation strategies
- Control recommendations
- Best practices
- Industry standards

### Similar Risk Identification
AI finds related risks:
- In your risk register
- From knowledge base
- Cross-organization (anonymized)

## Using the Assistant

### Ask Questions
Natural language queries:
- "What controls mitigate data breach risks?"
- "Show me high-severity risks in production"
- "Suggest treatment for ransomware risk"
- "Find similar risks to this one"

### Risk Analysis
1. Open any risk
2. Click **AI Analysis**
3. Review AI recommendations
4. Accept or modify suggestions

### Batch Analysis
Analyze multiple risks:
1. Select risks in register
2. Click **AI Analyze**
3. Review recommendations
4. Apply as appropriate

## AI Suggestions

### Accepting Suggestions
When AI makes a recommendation:
1. Review the suggestion
2. Click **Accept** to apply
3. Or **Modify** to adjust
4. Or **Dismiss** to ignore

### Feedback
Improve AI accuracy:
- Rate suggestions
- Provide feedback
- Correct errors
- AI learns from feedback

## Configuration

### AI Settings
Configure in **Settings → AI Configuration**:

- **Enable AI Features**: Toggle AI on/off
- **Auto-Suggest**: Automatic suggestions
- **Confidence Threshold**: Minimum confidence for suggestions
- **Data Sharing**: Anonymous data for improvement

### Privacy
Control what AI accesses:
- Risk descriptions
- Treatment plans
- Historical data
- Anonymized benchmarks

## Best Practices

### Using AI Effectively
- Provide detailed risk descriptions
- Review all suggestions
- Use as a starting point
- Human judgment final

### Improving Accuracy
- Consistent terminology
- Complete risk details
- Feedback on suggestions
- Regular data quality

### Appropriate Use
- AI assists, doesn't replace
- Verify critical decisions
- Understand limitations
- Maintain accountability

## Limitations

### AI Cannot
- Make final risk decisions
- Access external systems
- Guarantee accuracy
- Replace human expertise

### When to Not Use AI
- Highly sensitive risks
- Regulatory determinations
- Legal decisions
- Complex scenarios requiring context
