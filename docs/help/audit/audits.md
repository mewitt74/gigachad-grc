# Audit Management

Manage internal and external compliance audits, track readiness, and coordinate with auditors.

## Overview

The Audit module supports:
- Internal audit planning and execution
- External audit coordination
- Auditor portal access
- Request tracking
- Finding management

## Audit Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Internal | Self-assessments by your team |
| External | Third-party examinations |

   - Audit lead

## Audit Workflow

1. **Planning**: Define scope, schedule activities
2. **Fieldwork**: Execute tests, collect evidence
3. **Draft Report**: Compile findings
4. **Complete**: Finalize and distribute

## Evidence Requests

Track what auditors need:
1. Create requests with due dates
2. Assign to evidence owners
3. Track submission status
4. Review and accept/reject

## Auditor Portal

For external audits:
1. Enable portal access in audit settings
2. Invite auditors by email
3. Auditors can view scope, download evidence
